Summary: Latitude vs Max Temp

As anticipated, the closer one is to the equator, weather is increasing warmer than it is further away. The northern hemisphere doesn't appear to be as warm as the southern hemisphere during this time of year. The earth's rotation and axis tilt could be a contributing factor in this determination.
