Summary: Latitude vs Humidity

It can be concluded from the data that there is little correlation between humidity and latitude. However, there is are a concentration of cities at 60% humidity or above at various latitudes. Cities are also plotted across the map with a few cities at low humidity levels. Overall, cities display varying humidity across all latitudes.
